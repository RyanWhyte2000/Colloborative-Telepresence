Negative Cannotation of the utilization of collaborative telepresence.

Source: Negative Cannotation

Even though collaborative telepresence can be used to solve alot of problems in this current world, it can also cause several problems. Collaborative telepresence is responsible for the rise in many of the disruptive technologies we see today. Disruptive technologies is any new technology that alters the way businesses or industries function. Examples of disruptive technology include personal computers, which has displaced typewriters, email , which has disrupt the postal card and mailing industry, cellphones, which has disrupted the telecommunication industry, laptop, which later disrupted the personal computer industry, smartphone like laptops later disrupted the cell phone industry.
Collaborative Telepresence eliminates the need for person to be in the same physical location. This can cause persons to be socially awkward as no communication skills is being developed.
Persons that have little to no internet connections wont be able to effectively communicate with others. This can be very disasterous in industry such as banking sector and medicine.
