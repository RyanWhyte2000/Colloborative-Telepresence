Impact Of Collaborative Telepresence On Education

Source: HealthCare



Education is just one of the latest industry that has been drastically impacted by Collaborative Telepresence. THe abiltiy for students to receive an education without being in the same room is a ground-breaking delevopment in the education industry. Collaborative Telepresence has lead rise to a term called 'Online Learning'. Online learning is learning that takes place over the internet. With online learning students:

  1. Can be Flexible

    2. There are some students who work on the side while they attend school. Students who choose online learning can create their own schedule and not depend on a fixed class and time settings. This allows for students to engage in other extra curricular activities.

  2. Can Reduce Costs

    3. The cost for transportation to and from the education facility is greatly reduced with online learning.


  3. Better access information

    4. Online learning provides a wide variety of information. This is because Online learning is linked with the internet and the internet provides an extremely wide range of information.


  4. Can Increase 'One on One' time with tutors

    5. With online learning, Students can have more individual time with tutors as opposed to when they're in a classroom settings.
